# Blender Projects – 3D Modeling & Visualization

This repository contains my 3D modeling and animation projects created in **Blender**.

The works focus on technical visualization, object construction, scene composition and animated presentation.  
They demonstrate my ability to transform complex structures into clear and understandable visual experiences.

---

## Included Projects

### SSD – Internal Structure & Assembly
A technical visualization of a solid-state drive (SSD).

The project models individual hardware components and presents them through an assembly animation.  
The goal is to make the internal structure understandable and visually accessible.
